It ensures data integrity, security, and concurrency control. Popular types include relational (RDBMS), object-oriented, and NoSQL databases.

Power BI

Power BI is a business analytics tool by Microsoft that allows users to visualize and share insights from their data. It connects to a wide range of data sources, including databases, Excel, and cloud services. Power BI enables users to create interactive reports and dashboards with drag-and-drop functionality.
